Welcome to the workshop

A seminar to support girls in tech with community, education, and inspiration.

There will be three workshops, covering three different roles in technology. Students will attend all workshops to understand the individual roles, and how they work together.

  • Systems Analyst (SA)
  • Software Developer (Dev)
  • Systems Test Engineer (STE)
